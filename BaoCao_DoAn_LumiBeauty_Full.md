# 1. Mo dau

Trong boi canh thuong mai dien tu phat trien manh, nhu cau tu dong hoa khau tu van, gioi thieu san pham va tiep nhan don hang ngay cang tro nen quan trong. Doi voi cac cua hang my pham, khach hang thuong co nhu cau duoc tu van san pham theo tinh trang da, muc dich su dung va ngan sach. Neu phu thuoc hoan toan vao nhan vien tu van thu cong, qua trinh phan hoi de bi cham, kho mo rong va kho dam bao tinh san sang 24/7.

Tu thuc te do, de tai "Xay dung chatbot tu van va ho tro dat hang my pham tren nen tang Telegram tich hop AI" duoc de xuat nham xay dung mot he thong chat tu dong co kha nang:

- giao tiep truc tiep voi khach hang tren Telegram;
- ghi nhan thong tin nguoi dung;
- xac dinh loai da de goi y san pham phu hop;
- tim kiem, liet ke va hien thi thong tin san pham;
- ho tro dat hang theo quy trinh tung buoc;
- ket hop AI de nang cao chat luong tu van.

De tai khong chi mang tinh ung dung thuc tien ma con cho thay kha nang ket hop giua chatbot, co so du lieu NoSQL, API ben thu ba va dich vu cloud trong mot bai toan kinh doanh cu the. Diem noi bat cua du an la huong den trai nghiem nguoi dung don gian, than thien, de tiep can, trong khi van dam bao tinh mo rong va kha nang nang cap trong tuong lai.

`[Hinh 1.1]: Chen anh giao dien tong quan bot tren Telegram`

# 2. Tong quan de tai

## 2.1. Ten bai toan

Bai toan dat ra la xay dung mot chatbot ho tro ban my pham tren Telegram, trong do bot co the thay the mot phan vai tro cua nhan vien cham soc khach hang o cac tac vu co tinh lap di lap lai, gom:

- chao mung va huong dan nguoi dung;
- thu thap thong tin co ban cua khach hang;
- tu van san pham theo loai da;
- tim kiem san pham theo tu khoa;
- ho tro xem thong tin chi tiet va hinh anh san pham;
- tao va luu don hang;
- tra loi cac cau hoi tu do bang AI.

## 2.2. Muc tieu de tai

Muc tieu tong quat cua de tai la xay dung mot chatbot tu van my pham thong minh, de su dung, co kha nang ho tro nguoi dung tu giai doan tim hieu san pham den giai doan dat hang.

Muc tieu cu the:

- Xay dung bot Telegram bang Python.
- Ket noi bot voi co so du lieu Azure Cosmos DB de luu tru users, products va orders.
- Xay dung cac chuc nang co ban cua mot he thong ban hang qua chatbot.
- Tich hop Gemini AI de tra loi cac cau hoi tu van theo van phong tu nhien.
- To chuc ma nguon theo cau truc ro rang, de bao tri va mo rong.

## 2.3. Doi tuong va pham vi nghien cuu

De tai tap trung vao doi tuong nguoi dung la khach hang co nhu cau tim hieu va mua my pham thong qua Telegram. Pham vi he thong trong phien ban hien tai gom:

- bot tu van tren Telegram;
- co so du lieu luu tru thong tin khach hang, san pham, don hang;
- AI ho tro tu van;
- cac san pham duoc seed san trong database de phuc vu demo;
- co che van hanh local va san sang trien khai len moi truong cloud.

He thong chua tap trung vao cac noi dung nhu thanh toan truc tuyen, quan tri admin tren web, phan quyen nhan vien, thong ke doanh thu hoac dong bo voi he thong ERP.

## 2.4. Y nghia thuc tien

De tai co y nghia thuc tien cao vi mo phong mot kich ban ban hang rat pho bien: tu van, gioi thieu san pham va nhan don qua nen tang nhan tin. Mo hinh nay co the ap dung cho cua hang my pham nho, shop online, spa, nha thuoc da lieu hoac cac he thong can tro ly ao CSKH co chi phi thap.

Ben canh gia tri ung dung, de tai con co y nghia hoc thuat o cho no tong hop duoc nhieu mang kien thuc da hoc trong mot san pham cu the. Sinh vien khong chi viet mot chuong trinh don le ma phai ket hop:

- lap trinh huong module trong Python;
- ket noi API ben thu ba;
- to chuc va luu tru du lieu thuc te;
- xay dung luong hoi dap co trang thai;
- tich hop AI vao bai toan nghiep vu;
- dinh huong trien khai ung dung tren cloud.

Chinh vi vay, de tai phu hop voi tinh than cua mot do an ung dung: co bai toan thuc te, co san pham chay duoc, co du lieu, co kha nang demo va co tiem nang mo rong sau khi hoan thanh mon hoc.

# 3. Phan tich yeu cau he thong

## 3.1. Yeu cau chuc nang

Thong qua viec phan tich ma nguon hien co, he thong can dap ung cac nhom chuc nang sau:

### 3.1.1. Chuc nang khoi dong va gioi thieu bot

Khi nguoi dung nhap lenh `/start`, bot se:

- ghi nhan nguoi dung vao container `users` neu chua ton tai;
- gui loi chao;
- hien thi danh sach cac lenh ho tro.

### 3.1.2. Chuc nang xac dinh loai da

Nguoi dung su dung lenh `/skintype` de chon loai da qua cac nut bam inline. Sau khi lua chon, bot cap nhat du lieu vao database va su dung thong tin nay lam co so goi y san pham.

### 3.1.3. Chuc nang goi y san pham

Khi nguoi dung go lenh `/recommend`, bot:

- doc loai da da luu trong `users`;
- truy van container `products`;
- loc cac san pham co truong `skintype` phu hop;
- tra ve danh sach san pham de xuat.

### 3.1.4. Chuc nang xem danh sach san pham

Lenh `/products` cho phep nguoi dung xem san pham theo danh muc. He thong su dung cac nut inline de chia san pham theo nhom:

- lam sach va tay trang;
- toner va xit khoang;
- serum va tinh chat;
- kem duong va mat;
- kem chong nang;
- mat na va nhom khac;
- xem tat ca san pham.

### 3.1.5. Chuc nang tim kiem san pham

Lenh `/search <tu khoa>` cho phep tim san pham theo ten hoac mo ta. Chuc nang nay ho tro nguoi dung tim nhanh san pham trong tap du lieu lon hon.

### 3.1.6. Chuc nang xem anh san pham

Lenh `/xem <ten san pham>` duoc su dung de tra ve hinh anh minh hoa cung mo ta ngan gon cua san pham. He thong uu tien doc anh tu thu muc noi bo cua du an, sau do moi su dung nguon du phong.

### 3.1.7. Chuc nang dat hang

Lenh `/order` kich hoat quy trinh dat hang theo kieu hoi dap tung buoc. Bot lan luot:

- yeu cau nhap ten san pham;
- yeu cau nhap so luong;
- yeu cau nhap ten nguoi nhan;
- yeu cau nhap dia chi;
- hien thi thong tin tong hop;
- cho phep xac nhan hoac huy don.

Neu nguoi dung xac nhan, he thong tao don hang moi trong container `orders`.

### 3.1.8. Chuc nang xem lich su don hang

Lenh `/myorder` cho phep nguoi dung xem danh sach don hang gan day da dat, sap xep theo thoi gian tao.

### 3.1.9. Chuc nang tu van AI

He thong ho tro hai hinh thuc:

- lenh `/ask <cau hoi>`;
- nhan tin tu do khong bat dau bang dau `/`.

AI duoc cau hinh de dong vai tro nhan vien tu van my pham, dua tren danh sach san pham hien co trong database. Cac truong hop muon mua hang se duoc huong dan quay ve luong `/order`, tranh de AI xac nhan don thay cho logic he thong.

## 3.2. Yeu cau phi chuc nang

Ngoai chuc nang nghiep vu, he thong can dap ung cac tieu chi phi chuc nang sau:

- Giao dien than thien, de su dung tren Telegram.
- Ma nguon to chuc theo module, de bao tri.
- De mo rong them chuc nang, them san pham, them model AI.
- Ho tro luu tru du lieu linh hoat bang NoSQL.
- Co kha nang van hanh local va trien khai cloud.
- Toc do phan hoi nhanh voi cac lenh co ban.

## 3.3. Tac nhan su dung

Tac nhan chinh cua he thong la:

- Khach hang: nguoi tro chuyen voi bot, nhan tu van, tim san pham va dat hang.
- Quan tri vien / nha phat trien: cau hinh bot, cap nhat san pham, seed du lieu, van hanh he thong.

## 3.4. Quy trinh nghiep vu tong quat

Xet theo goc nhin nghiep vu, he thong van hanh theo mot quy trinh tuong doi ro rang:

1. Khach hang bat dau tro chuyen voi bot.
2. Bot huong dan khach lua chon loai da.
3. Du lieu loai da duoc luu lai de phuc vu ca nhan hoa.
4. Khach co the xem san pham, tim kiem hoac hoi AI.
5. Khi da chon duoc san pham phu hop, khach kich hoat luong dat hang.
6. Bot thu thap day du thong tin don.
7. Don hang duoc luu vao database de phuc vu theo doi va xu ly ve sau.

Quy trinh nay rat gan voi hoat dong cua mot cua hang online quy mo nho. Nhờ do, de tai khong bi ly thuyet hoa qua muc ma bám rat sat thuc te.

## 3.5. Mo ta dau vao - dau ra cua he thong

### 3.5.1. Dau vao

Dau vao cua he thong bao gom:

- tin nhan van ban tu nguoi dung tren Telegram;
- lua chon nut bam inline;
- thong tin cau hinh trong file moi truong;
- danh sach san pham duoc nap tu tep seed vao database.

### 3.5.2. Dau ra

Dau ra cua he thong bao gom:

- thong diep huong dan;
- danh sach san pham duoc dinh dang de doc;
- hinh anh minh hoa san pham;
- phan hoi AI;
- ma don hang va lich su don hang cua nguoi dung.

## 3.6. Rang buoc va gia dinh cua de tai

Trong pham vi de tai, mot so rang buoc duoc chap nhan de phu hop quy mo mon hoc:

- nguoi dung tu khai bao thong tin giao hang, he thong chua xac minh;
- san pham hien la du lieu mau phuc vu demo;
- chua co thanh toan online;
- AI chi lam vai tro tu van ho tro;
- chua co admin panel de chinh sua du lieu truc tiep.

Nhung rang buoc nay la hop ly vi muc tieu cua de tai la chung minh duoc mo hinh he thong va luong xu ly chinh, chua bat buoc dat den cap do van hanh thuong mai hoan chinh.

# 4. Kien truc va thiet ke he thong

## 4.1. Kien truc tong the

He thong duoc xay dung theo mo hinh tach lop tuong doi ro rang:

1. Lop giao tiep nguoi dung: Telegram chat.
2. Lop ung dung: bot Python xu ly lenh, callback, hoi dap va AI.
3. Lop du lieu: Azure Cosmos DB.
4. Lop AI dich vu ben ngoai: Google Gemini API.

So do mo ta:

Khach hang -> Telegram Bot API -> ung dung Python -> Azure Cosmos DB / Gemini API

`[Hinh 2.1]: Chen so do kien truc tong the`

## 4.2. Cau truc thu muc du an

Cau truc thu muc thuc te cua du an gom cac thanh phan chinh:

- `app.py`: diem khoi dong ung dung.
- `bot/`: xu ly cac lenh, keyboard, thong diep, AI.
- `database/`: xu ly ket noi va thao tac voi Cosmos DB.
- `data/`: seed du lieu san pham va cac tep ho tro.
- `img/`, `images/`: anh san pham.
- `requirements.txt`: danh sach thu vien.
- `Dockerfile`: dong goi trien khai.

`[Hinh 4.1]: Chen anh cay thu muc du an`

## 4.3. Nguyen ly hoat dong

Khi nguoi dung gui mot lenh hoac mot tin nhan:

1. Telegram chuyen ban tin ve bot.
2. Ung dung Python tiep nhan va chuyen den handler phu hop.
3. Handler co the:
   - doc/ghi du lieu tu Cosmos DB;
   - lay thong tin san pham;
   - goi AI de sinh cau tra loi;
   - tra ket qua lai cho nguoi dung.

## 4.4. Thiet ke module

### 4.4.1. Module `app.py`

Day la noi khoi tao `ApplicationBuilder`, dang ky cac `CommandHandler`, `CallbackQueryHandler`, `MessageHandler` va `ConversationHandler`. Ung dung ho tro hai che do:

- `run_polling()` khi phat trien local;
- `run_webhook()` khi trien khai production.

Dieu nay cho thay he thong duoc thiet ke linh hoat, phu hop ca demo noi bo lan trien khai thuc te.

### 4.4.2. Module `bot/handlers.py`

Day la module trung tam cua nghiep vu. Tat ca luong lenh chinh deu duoc xu ly tai day, bao gom:

- `start`
- `skintype`
- `recommend`
- `product_list`
- `search`
- `view_image`
- `my_orders`
- `ask_ai`
- `normal_chat`
- luong hoi dap dat hang

### 4.4.3. Module `bot/keyboards.py`

Module nay dinh nghia cac ban phim inline, giup nang cao trai nghiem nguoi dung. Thay vi bat nguoi dung nhap chinh xac van ban, he thong cho phep chon nut, giam sai sot va tang tinh truc quan.

### 4.4.4. Module `bot/messages.py`

Module nay tach rieng cac noi dung thong diep khoi logic xu ly, giup code de doc, de bao tri va de sua doi noi dung giao tiep.

### 4.4.5. Module `bot/ai.py`

Module nay xu ly ket noi Gemini AI. Diem dang chu y la prompt duoc thiet ke de:

- dong vai tro chuyen gia tu van my pham;
- chi duoc tu van dua tren danh sach san pham cua cua hang;
- neu khach muon mua thi bat buoc huong dan lenh `/order`;
- gioi han do dai cau tra loi.

Day la cach tiep can hop ly de AI khong tra loi lan man va khong vuot qua luong nghiep vu chinh.

### 4.4.6. Module `database/`

Nhom module trong thu muc `database/` duoc tach theo tung doi tuong du lieu:

- `cosmos.py`: tao client, database, container.
- `users.py`: doc/ghi thong tin nguoi dung.
- `products.py`: truy van san pham.
- `orders.py`: tao don va doc lich su don.

Kieu to chuc nay dam bao tinh tach biet giua xu ly nghiep vu va xu ly du lieu.

## 4.5. Phan tich luong du lieu trong he thong

Luong du lieu cua he thong co the chia thanh 3 nhom chinh:

### 4.5.1. Luong du lieu nguoi dung

Ngay khi nguoi dung bat dau su dung bot, he thong sinh ho so co ban gom `telegram_id`, `username` va `skintype`. Du lieu nay phuc vu cho ca nhan hoa va truy xuat lich su tuong tac toi thieu.

### 4.5.2. Luong du lieu san pham

San pham duoc nap truoc vao `products` bang script seed. Trong qua trinh van hanh, cac lenh `/recommend`, `/products`, `/search`, `/xem` deu doc tu tap du lieu nay. Day la nguon thong tin trung tam cua he thong.

### 4.5.3. Luong du lieu don hang

Sau khi khach hoan tat luong `/order`, he thong tao mot ban ghi don hang moi. Du lieu don hang la dau ra nghiep vu gia tri nhat cua bot vi no phan anh ket qua chuyen doi tu tu van sang mua hang.

## 4.6. Nguyen tac thiet ke duoc ap dung

Qua cau truc ma nguon, co the nhan thay du an da ap dung mot so nguyen tac thiet ke phan mem quan trong:

- Tach rieng noi dung thong diep khoi logic xu ly.
- Tach database access theo tung loai doi tuong.
- Han che viec tron lan giua nghiep vu va tang luu tru.
- Uu tien tao cac ham chuc nang nho, de hieu.
- Duy tri mot diem vao duy nhat la `app.py`.

Nhung nguyen tac nay tuy don gian nhung rat quan trong doi voi mot de tai sinh vien, vi no cho thay nhom khong chi "lam cho chay" ma con co y thuc to chuc he thong.

# 5. Phan tich va mo ta chuc nang

## 5.1. Chuc nang chao mung

Khi nguoi dung bat dau tro chuyen bang lenh `/start`, bot se:

- luu `telegram_id`, `username`, `skintype = None` neu day la nguoi dung moi;
- gui loi chao va danh sach lenh.

Muc dich cua chuc nang nay la khoi tao ho so nguoi dung va huong dan cach su dung bot ngay tu dau.

## 5.2. Chuc nang xac dinh loai da

Nguoi dung nhan `/skintype`, bot hien thi 4 lua chon:

- da dau;
- da kho;
- da hon hop;
- da nhay cam.

Sau khi nguoi dung chon, bot cap nhat du lieu vao container `users`. Tu do, he thong co co so ca nhan hoa goi y san pham.

`[Hinh 3.1]: Chen anh giao dien chon loai da`

## 5.3. Chuc nang goi y san pham

Lenh `/recommend` la mot trong nhung chuc nang cot loi. Quy trinh xu ly:

1. Kiem tra xem nguoi dung da chon loai da hay chua.
2. Neu chua, bot yeu cau quay lai `/skintype`.
3. Neu da co, bot truy van cac san pham co `ARRAY_CONTAINS(c.skintype, @s)`.
4. Bot hien thi danh sach san pham phu hop.

Chuc nang nay giup tao su lien ket giua ho so khach hang va san pham, mo phong duoc quy trinh tu van ca nhan hoa.

## 5.4. Chuc nang xem danh sach san pham theo danh muc

Lenh `/products` giup nguoi dung kham pha danh muc san pham. He thong phan nhom dua tren tu khoa trong ten san pham. Cac san pham co the duoc tra ve kem hinh anh neu tep anh ton tai trong thu muc du an.

Day la diem thuc te va huu ich vi nguoi dung khong can nho ten chinh xac san pham van co the xem va lua chon.

`[Hinh 3.2]: Chen anh giao dien danh muc san pham`

## 5.5. Chuc nang tim kiem san pham

Nguoi dung co the tim theo tu khoa bang `/search serum`, `/search Cetaphil` hoac `/search chong nang`. He thong su dung cau truy van `CONTAINS(LOWER(c.name), @k)` va `CONTAINS(LOWER(c.description), @k)` de tim tren ten va mo ta.

Loi ich cua chuc nang nay:

- tim nhanh san pham;
- giam so buoc thao tac;
- phu hop voi nguoi dung da co nhu cau cu the.

## 5.6. Chuc nang xem anh san pham

Lenh `/xem <tu khoa>` giup nguoi dung xem minh hoa mot san pham cu the. He thong uu tien lay anh theo thu tu:

1. file anh map san trong du an;
2. file anh trong thu muc `images/`;
3. anh mac dinh;
4. URL anh;
5. URL du phong theo nhom san pham.

Co che nay cho thay nhom da tinh den tinh on dinh cua trai nghiem nguoi dung, tranh truong hop bot mat kha nang hien thi chi vi thieu mot file anh.

## 5.7. Chuc nang dat hang

Quy trinh dat hang la mot luong hoi dap co trang thai. Day la phan quan trong vi no cho thay he thong khong chi dung o muc tu van ma da di den xu ly nghiep vu giao dich.

### 5.7.1. Cac buoc dat hang

1. Nguoi dung nhap `/order`.
2. Bot yeu cau nhap ten san pham.
3. Bot kiem tra san pham co ton tai khong.
4. Bot hoi so luong.
5. Bot hoi ten nguoi nhan.
6. Bot hoi dia chi giao hang.
7. Bot hien thi tong ket.
8. Nguoi dung bam xac nhan hoac huy.
9. Neu xac nhan, bot tao ban ghi moi trong `orders`.

### 5.7.2. Y nghia cua luong hoi dap

ConversationHandler giup he thong quan ly duoc trang thai cua tung nguoi dung. Moi tin nhan tiep theo duoc hieu dung vai tro cua no trong boi canh don hang hien tai. Day la giai phap rat phu hop cho chatbot nghiep vu.

`[Hinh 2.2]: Chen so do luong xu ly dat hang`

`[Hinh 3.4]: Chen anh man hinh xac nhan don hang`

## 5.8. Chuc nang xem lich su don hang

Khi nguoi dung nhap `/myorder`, he thong truy van don theo `user_id`, sap xep theo `created_at` giam dan va gioi han 10 don gan nhat. Chuc nang nay nang cao tinh minh bach va trai nghiem hau mua.

## 5.9. Chuc nang tu van AI

He thong su dung Gemini AI de tra loi cac cau hoi ve da va san pham. Diem manh cua phan nay nam o prompt engineering:

- AI khong tu van vo han;
- AI dua tren danh sach san pham that cua shop;
- AI khong thay the quy trinh dat hang;
- AI tra loi ngan gon, de doc, than thien.

Voi cach thiet ke nay, AI dong vai tro bo tro nghiep vu, khong pha vo luong xu ly chinh cua he thong.

`[Hinh 3.3]: Chen anh giao dien hoi dap AI`

## 5.10. Mo ta chi tiet quy trinh xu ly mot so lenh tieu bieu

### 5.10.1. Luong xu ly lenh `/start`

- Nguoi dung gui lenh.
- Bot lay thong tin `effective_user`.
- He thong kiem tra su ton tai cua user trong database.
- Neu user chua ton tai, bot tao moi ban ghi.
- Bot gui thong diep chao mung va danh sach lenh.

Luong xu ly nay tuy ngan gon nhung la buoc khoi tao rat quan trong, giup nhung lenh sau co du lieu nen de su dung.

### 5.10.2. Luong xu ly lenh `/recommend`

- Bot doc `user_id`.
- He thong lay `skintype` tu container `users`.
- Truy van san pham phu hop trong container `products`.
- Dinh dang thong tin san pham.
- Gui danh sach goi y cho nguoi dung.

Day la luong the hien ro tinh ca nhan hoa cua he thong.

### 5.10.3. Luong xu ly lenh `/search`

- Bot doc tu khoa tu tham so nguoi dung.
- Chuyen tu khoa ve chu thuong.
- Truy van du lieu theo ten va mo ta.
- Hien thi danh sach ket qua.

Luong nay cho thay bot khong phu thuoc hoan toan vao menu co dinh ma da co kha nang tra cuu du lieu linh hoat.

### 5.10.4. Luong xu ly lenh `/order`

# 6. Thiet ke du lieu va co so du lieu

## 6.1. Lua chon Azure Cosmos DB

Du an su dung Azure Cosmos DB for NoSQL de luu tru du lieu. Day la lua chon phu hop vi:

- du lieu san pham va don hang duoi dang JSON rat linh hoat;
- de mo rong khi so luong ban ghi tang;
- thao tac doc/ghi nhanh;
- thuan tien cho ung dung Python.

## 6.2. Cac container chinh

### 6.2.1. Container `users`

Luu thong tin co ban cua nguoi dung Telegram:

- `id`
- `telegram_id`
- `username`
- `skintype`

Partition key: `/telegram_id`

### 6.2.2. Container `products`

Luu danh sach san pham:

- `id`
- `name`
- `price`
- `skintype`
- `description`
- `image`
- `image_url`

Partition key: `/id`

### 6.2.3. Container `orders`

Luu thong tin don hang:

- `id`
- `user_id`
- `customer_name`
- `product_name`
- `quantity`
- `address`
- `status`
- `created_at`

Partition key: `/user_id`

`[Hinh 5.1]: Chen so do mo hinh du lieu users - products - orders`

## 6.3. Seed du lieu

Du an co tep `data/seed_products.py` dung de nap san danh sach san pham mau vao database. Day la buoc quan trong de:

- tao du lieu demo nhanh;
- kiem thu cac chuc nang recommend, products, search;
- mo phong cua hang co san catalog.

Tap du lieu trong du an gom nhieu nhom san pham khac nhau nhu sua rua mat, toner, serum, kem duong, kem chong nang, mat na, tay trang va mot so san pham dac tri.

## 6.4. Uu diem cua thiet ke du lieu

- Linh hoat khi them truong moi.
- Phu hop voi cau truc san pham khong dong nhat.
- De ket hop hinh anh, mo ta, loai da.
- Tien cho viec mo rong sau nay nhu them rating, ton kho, thuong hieu.

## 6.5. Ly do lua chon partition key

Trong Cosmos DB, partition key anh huong den hieu nang va kha nang mo rong. Nhìn vao du an, co the thay cach chon partition key mang tinh hop ly:

- `users` dung `/telegram_id` vi moi user gan voi mot nguoi dung duy nhat;
- `products` dung `/id` vi moi san pham la mot tai lieu doc lap;
- `orders` dung `/user_id` vi viec tra cuu lich su don theo nguoi dung la nghiep vu xay ra thuong xuyen.

Viec lua chon nay cho thay nhom da co su quan tam den dac thu truy van, khong chi don thuần tao container de luu du lieu.

## 6.6. Phan tich truy van du lieu

Du an dang su dung mot so mau truy van tieu bieu:

- truy van theo id hoac partition key;
- truy van tim san pham theo loai da bang `ARRAY_CONTAINS`;
- truy van tim theo ten va mo ta bang `CONTAINS`;
- truy van lich su don hang theo `user_id` sap xep theo thoi gian.

Nhung truy van nay tuy khong qua phuc tap nhung phan anh dung ban chat cua ung dung chatbot ban hang: doc nhanh, loc nhanh, uu tien tra ket qua cho nguoi dung.

## 6.7. Huong mo rong mo hinh du lieu

Neu phat trien trong tuong lai, co the bo sung them cac truong:

- `brand`: thuong hieu san pham;
- `stock`: ton kho;
- `rating`: diem danh gia;
- `sold_count`: so lan ban;
- `category`: danh muc chuan hoa;
- `ingredients`: thanh phan noi bat;
- `usage`: huong dan su dung;
- `contraindications`: luu y khi dung.

Khi bo sung nhung truong tren, chatbot se co the tu van sau hon va tro thanh mot he thong ban hang thong minh hon.
